Global Warming
vs.
Climate Change

There is a difference but similar results!

Climate Change is a long-term change in the average weather patterns that have come to define Earth’s local, regional and global climates. Whilst, Global Warming is the long-term heating of the Earth’s climate system due to human activities, primarily fossil fuel burning, which increases heat trapping greenhouse gas levels. Natural
Disasters:

They are getting stronger!

Warm water is important because it acts as a fuel for hurricanes. Water has to be at least 26 degrees Celsius for a storm to form. The warmer the water, the more a hurricane can resist forces that would cause it to weaken. The Atlantic is predicted to warm faster than the rest of the world's oceans.
